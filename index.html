Osamu Tezuka

The Father of Manga and Anime

Time line of Osamu Tezuka's life:

  • 1928 - Born in Osaka, Japan
  • 1944 - Tezuka started work in factory and simultaneosly continued writing manga
  • 1945 - He started studying medicine in Osaka University
  • 1946 - Tezuka published his first professional work: Diary of Ma-chan
  • 1948 - 1951 - He published a trilogy od science fiction: Lost World, Metropolis, Nextworld
  • 1950 - 1953 - He published his first major succes Kimba the White Lion
  • 1951 - Tezuka graduated from the Osaka Schoold Medicine and published Ambassador Atom
  • 1953 - He published Ribon no Kishi
  • 1960 - Released his film Alakazam tthe Great
  • 1963 - Started broadcast Tezuka's Astro Boy
  • 1967 - Tezuka created the magazine COM
  • 1967 - Published Dororo
  • 1989 - Osamu Tezuka died of stomach cancer in Tokyo
